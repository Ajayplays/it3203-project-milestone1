Project Overview

This website presents research on three major cybersecurity topics: malware, spoofing, and botnets. These threats affect businesses, schools, governments, and everyday users. The goal of this project is to explain what these threats are, how they work, and why understanding them matters in web development and information technology.

As more services move online, users must understand digital risks. Cyber attackers use malicious software, fake identities, and infected device networks to steal data, interrupt services, and trick people into unsafe actions. Learning about these threats helps users browse more safely and helps future IT professionals create stronger systems.
